Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What documents do I need to buy a car?

You'll need a valid ID, proof of address, and if financing, proof of income. For international buyers, passport and work permit may be required.

Do you offer test drives?

Yes, test drives are available by appointment. Contact us to schedule a test drive for any vehicle in our inventory.

What financing options are available?

We offer various financing options through our partner banks including installment plans, lease options, and balloon payment plans.

Do you deliver vehicles nationwide?

Yes, we deliver to all major cities in Kenya. Delivery within Nairobi is free, while other locations have a delivery fee based on distance.

What warranty comes with the cars?

All our vehicles come with a 12-month comprehensive warranty. Extended warranty options are also available.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Yes, we accept trade-ins. Contact us for a free valuation of your current vehicle.
